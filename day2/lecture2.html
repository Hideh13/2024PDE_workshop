Title: Singular SU(3) Toda systems on torus

Abstract: In this talk, I will survey our main results from a series of papers about singular SU(3) Toda systems on torus. For the non-critical case, we give a sharp upper bound of the number of solutions. For the critical case, we give the structure of even solutions.
